Course 04

Organizational Design for Product Teams

A seminar on Conway's Law, team topology, and the agentic era

Teams Strategy 15 Weeks UG + Graduate

Organizations are products. This course applies Conway's Law, the Rule of Seven, and platform team theory to real product org design — from four-person startups to four-hundred-person enterprises to the emerging challenge of designing organizations that include AI agents as collaborators. Students produce an original org design proposal and defend it in a mock board review.
